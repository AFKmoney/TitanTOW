## 🚀 The T.O.W. Architecture

Our proprietary **Ternary-Over-Windows (T.O.W.)** algorithm works silently in the background. It analyzes everything running on your system, scores it based on importance, and mathematically isolates processes into three strict zones:

1. **The Vault (+1) :** Your active applications (Games, Renders, Compiles). They get VIP access to dedicated cores and your CPU's L1/L2 cache. No interruptions.
2. **The Buffer (0) :** Windows OS and critical system files. They are allowed to run, but they cannot cross into your dedicated performance cores.
3. **The Quarantine (-1) :** Background noise, telemetry, and idle tasks. They are starved of resources and locked to the slowest threads on your system.
